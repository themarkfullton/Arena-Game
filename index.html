DREAMLANDS

A HP Lovecraft inspired arena game

Choose a fleshing to inhabit:

Adventurer

Surmounter of the unsurmountable and discoverer of the unknown. The Dreamlands lured you in with their promise of exploration and thrills.

[ Physical-Based; Well-Rounded ]

Outlaw

Always on the search for valuable secrets. Lured into the Dreamlands by whisperings of legendary treasure.

[ Physical Based; Quick and skilled but weak ]

Doctor

For the sake of others, you mastered the art of medicine. When an eerily supernatural plague crept into your city, however, you were forced to turn to the occult for answers.

[ Physical-Based; Access to healing abilities ]

Cultist

Worshipper of the Old Ones, you weave the fabric of reality at will. The Dreamlands are your regular walking grounds.

[ Mental-Based; Access to arcane abilities ]

Madman

Writer convinced that this hellscape is his own handiwork. Woke up in the Dreamlands after a long night of writing.

[ Mental-Based; Access to storytelling abilities ]

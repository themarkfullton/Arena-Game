Dreamlands

An HP Lovecraft Inspired Arena Game

“Wise men have interpreted dreams, and the gods have laughed”

-H.P. Lovecraft (“The Dreamland Series”)

Every night when we close our eyes, we begin a journey to an alternate—and eerie—universe. Those who remember such a journey can only refer to this universe under a cryptic name: The Dreamlands.

Occupying these lands are all manner of hellish creature, foreign architecture, and long-forgotten secrets. Treasure abounds in these lands—but only for those full-witted enough to seek it out. Death in these lands, after all, means a severing with one’s physical body.

Though you have met with many warnings, you have heeded none of them. For one reason or another, you have made your way into these Dreamlands. Can you survive them?
